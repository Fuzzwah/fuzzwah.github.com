Digital Photoframe

Objective: A computer located in our kitchen, housed in a photoframe to be used to keep up to date with news and weather, as well as listen to music.

Solution: Pulling apart an old laptop and reassembling it into the photoframe. Configuring a WinXP installation with a number of autoIT based scriped menus for use with an IR remote control.
